Calci - A Simple Math Calculator

Calculator Operations

A simple basic math calculator, which is a kind of similar to a small handheld calculator that perform operations such as addition, subtraction, division and multiplication.

The operations perfomed using this calculator is mentioned below:

  • Addition (+)
  • Subtraction (−)
  • Multiplication (×)
  • Division (÷)
